Together we create the future

On March 3, 2022, Russian occupation forces launched a missile strike onff, a small town on the outskirts of Kyiv. The rocket destroyed a house of culture, a village council building, two kindergartens, and other social and housing facilities. Another settlement of the Byshiv community was damaged - the village of Yasnogorodka, where fierce battles were fought with Russian invaders. Terrifying footage of destroyed houses (60), a school, a burned-out farm flew around the world. Recovery will require significant resources, and the community understands that state aid may come with significant delays, as well as to a limited extent. That is why the community decided to start preparations for renewal through the involvement of the world community - humanitarian foundations, sponsors, as well as concerned people from around the world
